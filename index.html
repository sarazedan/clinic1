Courtesy Shuttle
April 292018

The Clinic provides free handicap equipped shuttle service to pick up patients from major transportation hubs around the Clinic. This service, which has started in August, is made possible by a grant from Montgomery County. As you know, there is no public transportation available to and from the Clinic during non-rush hours and weekends. This service will operate from 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM on weekdays and 9:15 Am to 4:45 PM on weekends.

Open 7 Days A Week
April 292018

MCC Medical Clinic now opens seven days a week from 9 AM to 5 PM. The decision to extend operating days was prompted by the increasing demand for Clinic's various services. We are also expecting more volunteers to join us as most of them are available on weekends only. The clinic heavily depends on volunteer help, particularly in medical care area. If you are interested in volunteering for the Clinic, please contact our volunteer coordinator via email (volunteer_coordinator@mccclinic.org). Thank you.

Dental Care
April 292018

The newly constructed 2-chair dental clinic now operates four days a week, on Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Saturday. Montgomery County residents who are eligible for Montgomery Care program receive appointment priority. Clinic's own registered patients also receive appointment priority but all patients are welcomed on first come first served basis. The Clinic, which offers checkup, panoramic X-ray, and standard cleaning at deeply discounted rates, also offers many other preventive, corrective, and cosmetic dental care services. To find out more about our dental program, please call us at 301-384-2166 Ext 1001.

Our Mission
